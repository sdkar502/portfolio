Overview

Redesigning Government Services for Every New Yorker

Led end-to-end UI/UX design for the digital transformation of New York State's DMV web portal — redesigning online services for license status, address changes, driving records, and vehicle registration to be simple, accessible, and usable by all residents regardless of ability.

User Research Service Design WCAG 2.0 Compliance Figma Prototyping Sitecore CMS UAT Leadership HTML5 / CSS3 / Bootstrap
Challenge & Solution

The Challenge

The existing DMV portal had fragmented navigation, inaccessible form flows, poor mobile responsiveness, and significant WCAG 2.0 violations — creating barriers for residents with disabilities and high task abandonment rates across critical services.

The Solution

Conducted deep user research to map resident journeys, then redesigned service flows using component-based UI patterns, fully accessible HTML5 components, and ARIA-enhanced interactions — all validated through iterative UAT sessions with real users.

Overview

Accessible E-Commerce at Enterprise Scale

Served as UI/UX Designer and Web Accessibility Engineer for a large-scale e-commerce platform redesign — improving user experience across product browsing, cart, checkout, and account management while achieving full WCAG 2.0 A/AA and Section 508 compliance across all customer-facing touchpoints.

E-Commerce UX Accessibility Audit Responsive Design Figma / Adobe XD UAT & Research HTML5 / Bootstrap Angular Components

The Challenge

The existing e-commerce platform had inconsistent UI patterns, inaccessible dynamic content (cart updates, modals, product filters), poor mobile UX, and significant WCAG violations that excluded users with disabilities from completing purchases.

The Solution

Conducted user research and accessibility audits across all customer journeys, redesigned component-level UI patterns, and implemented accessible HTML5/ARIA components — validated through structured UAT sessions with real customers.
